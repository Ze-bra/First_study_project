The Statue of Liberty.

The statue is on an island that can be reached by ferry. There is a history museum that tells visitors about the creation of the monument.
Exceeding 354 steps, one can ascend to the level of the "Lady Liberty" crown and see a stunning panorama of New York Harbor.

Hollywood Walk of Fame.

Fans of Hollywood actors flock here from all over the world to touch the treasured stars with their own hands.
The Walk of Fame is more than 2,500 stars with the names of Hollywood celebrities, who were noted for their significant contribution to American cinema.

Mount Rushmore.

This mountain was glorified by the sculptor Gutzon Borglum, according to whose idea several hundred masons carved in the rock the 18-meter faces of the most famous American presidents: Thomas Jefferson, George Washington, Abraham Lincoln and Theodore Roosevelt.
